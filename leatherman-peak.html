Leatherman Peak

Leatherman Peak, at 12,228 feet above sea level is the second highest peak in Idaho and the Lost River Range. The peak is located in Salmon-Challis National Forest in Custer County. It is 4.5 mi southeast of Borah Peak, its line parent. It is also about 4.5 mi northeast from U.S. Route 92. Mileage: 5 Miles Elevation Gain: 4,000 Feet Time: 7 Hours
