Mount Whitney

Mount Whitney is the tallest mountain in California, standing at an elevation of 14,505 feet (4,421 m). It is also the highest peak in the contiguous United States and is located in the Sierra Nevada range on the border of Sequoia National Park and Inyo National Mileage: 21-22 Miles Elevation Gain: 6,100-6,600 Feet Time: 12-16 Hours to Summit
