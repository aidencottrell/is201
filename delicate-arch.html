Delicate Arch

The Delicate Arch hike is a 3.2-mile (round trip) moderately difficult trail in Arches National Park, featuring a 550-foot elevation gain and taking 2-3 hours. It is mostly unshaded, steep slickrock with a narrow ledge, offering iconic views. Hike early or late to avoid extreme, exposed midday heat. Mileage: 3 Miles Elevation Gain: 480 Feet Time: 3 Hours
